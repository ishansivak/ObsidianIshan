# Meshtastic

Meshtastic is a decentralized, off-grid mesh communication protocol.

## Core Architecture
- **Routing**: Managed flooding.
- **Topology**: Ad-hoc, spontaneous formation of mesh networks.
- **Constraints**: Limited to small hop counts (default 3, hard cap 7) to prevent spectrum exhaustion.
- **Use Cases**: Tactical teams, backcountry hiking, spontaneous community networks.

## Pros & Cons
- **Pros**: Easy to set up, requires no prior infrastructure, resilient to dynamic topology changes.
- **Cons**: "Chatty" nature can lead to spectrum congestion and high power consumption on edge devices.
