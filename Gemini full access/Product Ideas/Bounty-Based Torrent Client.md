# Bounty-Based Torrent Client

## Concept
A P2P torrent client that incentivizes the seeding of rare or specific files by allowing users to post bounties for seeders.

## Key Features
- **Bounty Requests**: Users can create a "bounty" for a specific file (identified by magnet link or info hash).
- **Incentivized Seeding**: Seeders are rewarded (e.g., via crypto micro-payments) for keeping rare files available until the bounty requirements are met.
- **Proof of Seeding**: Mechanism to verify that the seeder is actively serving the requested data.
- **Community Driven**: Helps preserve digital content that is at risk of being lost due to lack of seeders.

## Target Audience
- Archivists and digital preservationists.
- Users looking for rare or niche content that is currently unavailable on standard P2P networks.
