# Saito Detours

The spirit of our channel is rooted in the "Saito Detour"—the intentional deviation from the beaten path to explore, experiment, and refine. It is where craftsmanship meets curiosity. We do not just build tools; we explore how those tools reshape the human experience.

- **Curiosity**: Nothing is off-limits if it advances our understanding.
- **Refinement**: Every detour is a lesson, every failure is a data point.
- **Craft**: We build for longevity, not just for the immediate need.
