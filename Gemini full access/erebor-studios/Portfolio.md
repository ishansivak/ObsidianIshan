# Erebor Studios: Vision Portfolio

## Vision
To carve out the foundational tools for the next generation of decentralized compute and hardware-as-code engineering. We prioritize human-centric design, absolute transparency, and rigorous, dwarven-grade engineering standards.

## The Spirit of Erebor
"Erebor" represents the mountain of ultimate craftsmanship—the deep, enduring stronghold of precision engineering. Erebor Studios exists to forge complex systems into highly usable, human-centric, and beautifully structured realities. We believe that technology should be an extension of the human mind, crafted with absolute integrity.
