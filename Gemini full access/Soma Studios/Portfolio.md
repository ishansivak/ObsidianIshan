# Soma Studios: Vision Portfolio

## Vision
To build the foundational tools for the next generation of decentralized compute and hardware-as-code engineering. We prioritize human-centric design, absolute transparency, and rigorous engineering standards.

## The Essence of Soma
"Soma" represents the *essence*—the foundational nectar that fuels the body of decentralized compute. Soma Studios exists to distill complex systems into usable, human-centric, and rigorously engineered realities. We believe that technology should be an extension of the mind, not a barrier to it.
