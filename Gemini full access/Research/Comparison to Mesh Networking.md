# Comparison to Mesh Networking
Synthesis of biological models with digital mesh topologies.

## Key Comparisons
| Feature | Mycelial Networks | Digital Mesh Networks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Routing** | Dynamic, chemical-based | Proactive/Reactive, algorithmic |
| **Resilience** | High, organic self-healing | High, redundant pathing |
| **Scaling** | Organic growth | Planned infrastructure expansion |

## Synthesis
By applying mycelial growth patterns to mesh routing protocols, we can develop networks that adapt more efficiently to fluctuating node availability and demand.
