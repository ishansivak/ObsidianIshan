# Fungi as Biological Networks
Foundational research on biological resource allocation.

## Key Mechanisms
- **Nutrient Flow**: Dynamic, demand-driven distribution.
- **Node Specialization**: Specialized tips for exploration and transport.
- **Communication**: Chemical signaling across the network.

## Parallels to Decentralized Systems
The ability of fungal networks to self-heal and optimize paths without a central controller offers a potent paradigm for decentralized network management.
