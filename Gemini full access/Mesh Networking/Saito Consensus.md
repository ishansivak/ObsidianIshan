# Saito Consensus

Saito provides an economic layer for decentralized networks to solve bandwidth and infrastructure problems.

## Mechanism: "Routing Work"
- **Incentive**: Nodes are compensated for the work of routing data, not just block production.
- **Economic Decay**: Packet fees are halved for every hop, incentivizing shorter, more efficient routing paths.
- **Spam Prevention**: Making spam economically ruinous by requiring fees to scale with hop count.

## Potential for Mesh Networks
- **Infrastructure Deployment**: Programmatically incentivizes the deployment of high-efficiency "Repeater" infrastructure in trustless environments.
- **Bandwidth Management**: Aligns economic incentives to optimize topology and bandwidth allocation.
